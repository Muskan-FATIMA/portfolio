DonorConnect

Connecting Donors to Save Lives

DonorConnect is a comprehensive web platform that bridges the gap between blood donors and recipients. It allows users to register, submit blood requests, and receive notifications about new requests. The platform also features a feedback system to acknowledge and reward donors for their contributions.
